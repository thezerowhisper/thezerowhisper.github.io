About This Website

This platform provides free medical calculators designed for Indian clinical practice. Tools include emergency scoring systems like CURB-65, NEWS2, and GCS, along with drug dose calculators, pediatric tools, obstetric calculators, and chronic disease tools such as eGFR and HbA1c converters.

All calculators are based on Indian guidelines (NCDC, IAP, FOGSI) and international standards (WHO, NICE, BTS). No login is required and no data is stored.
