About This Website

MedCalc India provides free clinical calculators designed for Indian clinical practice. Tools cover emergency scoring (CURB-65, NEWS2, GCS), drug dosing, paediatric tools, obstetric calculators, and chronic disease tools like eGFR and HbA1c converters.

All calculators follow Indian guidelines (NCDC, IAP, FOGSI) and international standards (WHO, NICE, BTS). No login is required and no patient data is ever stored or transmitted.
