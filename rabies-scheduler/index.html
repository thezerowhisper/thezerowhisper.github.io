What to Do After a Dog Bite or Animal Scratch

1. Wash the wound immediately with soap and running water for at least 15 minutes.

2. Visit a doctor immediately — do not wait or observe the animal first.

3. Start ARV on Day 0 — the same day as the bite if possible.

4. For Category III exposures (deep bites, puncture wounds, licks on mucous membranes), Rabies Immunoglobulin (RIG) must be administered on Day 0 alongside the first ARV dose. Calculate your RIG dose by weight →

5. Complete all 4 ARV doses — missing doses significantly reduces protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many rabies injections are needed in India?

4 anti-rabies vaccine (ARV) injections are required — given on Days 0, 3, 7, and 28 after the first dose. This is the Essen regimen used across India.

What is the ARV schedule after a dog bite in India?

The ARV (anti-rabies vaccine) schedule after a dog bite in India is: Day 0 (immediately), Day 3, Day 7, and Day 28. Use this calculator to get your exact ARV injection dates.

What is the rabies vaccine schedule for 0 3 7 28 days?

The 0-3-7-28 schedule refers to the Indian Essen regimen where rabies vaccine doses are given on Day 0, Day 3, Day 7, and Day 28. This calculator automatically computes all four dates from your Day 0.

What if I miss a rabies vaccine dose?

Take the missed ARV dose as soon as possible and continue the remaining doses on the original schedule. Do not restart the series. Consult your doctor if unsure.

Is rabies 100% fatal without vaccination?

Yes — once symptoms of rabies appear, it is almost always fatal. Timely ARV post-exposure prophylaxis is fully effective in preventing the disease if started promptly after exposure.

Do I need rabies immunoglobulin (RIG) along with the ARV?

RIG is required for Category III exposures (deep bites, puncture wounds, licks on mucous membranes or broken skin). It is given only once on Day 0, alongside the first ARV dose.
