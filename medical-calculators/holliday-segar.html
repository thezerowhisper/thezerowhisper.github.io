The 4-2-1 Rule Explained

The Holliday-Segar method (1957) determined that daily water requirements approximate 100 mL per 100 kcal metabolised. This leads to the famous 100-50-20 rule for daily volume and 4-2-1 rule for hourly rates.

NICE 2015 Fluid Recommendations

  • Fluid of choice: Isotonic saline (0.9% NaCl) with 5% dextrose.
  • Add potassium: 20 mmol/L KCl unless hyperkalaemic or anuric.
  • Avoid: Hypotonic fluids (0.18% or 0.45% NaCl) as routine maintenance due to hyponatraemia risk.
