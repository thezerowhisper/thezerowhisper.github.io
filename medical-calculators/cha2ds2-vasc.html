ESC 2020 Anticoagulation Decision

The 2020 ESC AF guidelines provide sex-specific anticoagulation thresholds:

  • Men ≥2 / Women ≥3: Oral anticoagulation recommended (Class I).
  • Men 1 / Women 2: Oral anticoagulation should be considered (Class IIa).
  • Men 0 / Women 1: Anticoagulation not recommended.
