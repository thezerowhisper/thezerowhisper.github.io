MAP Formula & Interpretation

MAP = DBP + [ 1/3 × (SBP - DBP) ]
MAP = (SBP + 2×DBP) / 3

A normal MAP range is typically 70–100 mmHg. In critical care, a MAP of ≥65 mmHg is often targeted to maintain adequate organ perfusion.
