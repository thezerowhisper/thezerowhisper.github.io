Interpretation & Formulas

Bazett: QT / √RR
Fridericia: QT / RR^(1/3)
RR = 60 / Heart Rate (sec)

Normal QTc Cutoffs

  • Males: Normal ≤440 ms.
  • Females: Normal ≤460 ms.
  • Critical: >500 ms (High TdP Risk).
