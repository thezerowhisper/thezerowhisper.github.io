Guide to Pregnancy Due Date & Care

Calculating your Estimated Due Date (EDD) is essential for planning prenatal checkups, scans, and your baby’s arrival. In India, a standard full-term pregnancy is 40 weeks (280 days). Here is how it is calculated:

Calculation Methods

Naegele's Rule: The most common method adds 7 days to your LMP and adds 9 months. Our calculator refines this by adjusting for your menstrual cycle length.

Ultrasound (CRL): Performed in the first trimester (7-13 weeks), the Crown-Rump Length is the most accurate method, especially if periods are irregular.

Indian Diet & Government Support

Focus on iron-rich foods like Palak (Spinach) and proteins like Paneer and Dal. First-time mothers in India can benefit from the PMMVY Scheme, providing ₹5,000 in cash incentives for health and nutrition.
