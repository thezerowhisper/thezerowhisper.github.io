Formulas & Clinical Uses

Mosteller: √(Height × Weight / 3600)
DuBois: 0.007184 × H^0.725 × W^0.425

Clinical Significance

  • Oncology: Dosing for most cytotoxic agents.
  • Cardiology: Calculation of Cardiac Index (CO / BSA).
  • Renal: Normalising absolute GFR to standard body size (1.73m²).
