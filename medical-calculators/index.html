Evidence-Based Guidelines

Our tools are built using validated thresholds: Indian-specific BMI cutoffs (overweight ≥23), Rabies PEP 4-dose Essen regimen (NCDC India), and internationally recognized standards like BTS (CURB-65) and RCP (NEWS2). All calculations are performed client-side; no clinical data is stored on our servers.
