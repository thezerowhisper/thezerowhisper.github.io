🔴 Fever above 40°C or fever lasting more than 3 days
🔴 Fever in a baby under 3 months old — go immediately
🔴 Fever with stiff neck, rash, severe headache, or confusion
🔴 Child is unusually drowsy, difficult to wake, or very irritable

🔴 Accidental overdose — go to emergency immediately
⚠️ Do not exceed maximum dose. Paracetamol overdose causes serious liver damage — keep all medicines out of children's reach. Do not give more than 4 doses in 24 hours. Do not use alongside other medicines containing paracetamol (e.g. combination cold syrups).

Paracetamol Dosing in India

The standard paracetamol dose is 15 mg per kg body weight per dose, given every 4–6 hours as needed. The maximum single dose is 1000 mg (1g) for adults and the maximum daily dose is 4000 mg (4g) for adults and 75 mg/kg/day for children.

Common Paracetamol Brands in India

Syrups: Calpol (125mg/5ml and 250mg/5ml), Dolo Syrup (125mg/5ml), Metacin Syrup, Febrex Plus. Tablets: Dolo 650, Crocin 500, Metacin 500, Calpol 500. All contain the same active ingredient — paracetamol (acetaminophen).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the paracetamol dose for children in India?

15 mg per kg per dose, every 4–6 hours. Maximum 4 doses in 24 hours. For a 10 kg child: 150 mg per dose = 6 ml of Calpol 125mg/5ml or 3 ml of Calpol 250mg/5ml.

How many ml of Calpol syrup for my child?

Use this calculator — enter your child's weight and select the syrup concentration. The exact ml will be calculated automatically based on 15mg/kg dosing.

What is the maximum paracetamol dose per day?

For adults: 4000 mg (4g) per day — maximum 1000mg per dose, every 4–6 hours, not more than 4 doses in 24 hours. For children: 75 mg/kg/day up to a maximum of 4g/day.

Is Dolo 650 safe for children?

Dolo 650mg tablets are generally for adults or older children/adolescents above approximately 40 kg. For younger children, use weight-based syrup dosing with Calpol or Dolo syrup formulations.

Can paracetamol be given every 4 hours?

Yes — paracetamol can be given every 4–6 hours as needed. Do not give more than 4 doses in any 24-hour period regardless of the interval used.
