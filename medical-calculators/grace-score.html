Clinical Importance

The GRACE score is specifically endorsed by ESC 2020 guidelines for risk stratification in NSTEMI. It predicts both short-term (in-hospital) and mid-term (6-month) outcomes, guiding the timing of invasive angiography.

⚠ Medical Disclaimer: GRACE score is a prognostic aid. Clinical decisions must integrate ECG, troponins, and expert cardiologist assessment.
