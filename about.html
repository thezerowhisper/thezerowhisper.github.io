🏥 What is MedCalc India?

MedCalc India is a free, open-access suite of 26+ clinical calculators and decision-support tools designed for Indian healthcare professionals. Every tool is built with Indian clinical guidelines at its core — including those from NCDC, IAP, FOGSI, and international bodies like WHO, NICE, and BTS.

The site covers emergency scoring (CURB-65, NEWS2, GCS), paediatrics (growth charts, milestones, vaccine schedules), obstetrics (due date, ObsCalc), metabolic tools (eGFR, HbA1c, Child-Pugh), infectious disease (rabies PEP, ARV dose), and occupational health (spirometry, biomedical waste guide).

👨‍⚕️ Who Built This?

These tools are built and maintained by a practicing Indian physician with MBBS and AFIH qualifications, currently working in a government healthcare setting. The motivation is simple: quick-access, mobile-friendly clinical tools that work without internet login, without ads cluttering the experience, and without the complexity of proprietary hospital software.

Every calculator has been reviewed against the referenced guidelines before publication. Clinical accuracy is non-negotiable.

🔒 Privacy & Data

No patient data is ever transmitted or stored. All calculations happen locally in your browser. No login is required. Anonymous usage analytics are collected via Umami (a privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics platform) to help understand which tools are most useful.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer

All tools on this website are intended for use by qualified healthcare professionals as clinical decision-support aids only. They do not replace clinical judgement, patient history, or examination. Always verify calculations independently and apply them in the context of the individual patient. The developers accept no liability for clinical decisions made using these tools.
